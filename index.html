What is it?


Intswaps is for people who have a variable-rate loan and wish they had a fixed-rate one instead (or vice-versa.) Are you locked into an ARM (adjustable rate mortgage) and deterred by refinancing costs that average $1500 to $5000 according to Trulia? Intswaps is an easy, inexpensive way to swap your variable rate interest payment for a fixed rate payment on a month by month basis for as many or few monthly payments as you want.

You just post refundable escrow in an Ethereum smart contract of 0.2% of the loan principal amount that you want the interest rate to apply to. The decentralized trust system of the Ethereum Virtual Machine will ensure that you receive your escrow plus your profit or (loss) back at the end of the swap contract. Interested in learning more? Just go to our no-obligation quote page to see how it would work for you and what your payouts would be in response to future changes in interest rates.
